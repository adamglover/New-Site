Meet the Breed

You'd like to know more about our wonderful breed? Then you are in the right place.

We could go on for pages and pages and pages about what a wonderful breed the Finnish Lapphund is. We could also go on for a fair while about those little 'unique qualities' that make a Lapphund a lapphund!

Actually what would be far better is to hear directly from our members...those that have had lapphunds in their lives for years, those with more than one Lapphund, those with Males and Females and those who've only just welcomed a Lapphund into their lives! So pour yourself your drink of choice, put your feet up and pick your favourite from below... ok, we know you'll look at them all, but enjoy!
